How to Play

1

Create a Game

Click "Start Playing" to create your own game. Upload (or paste) images and give your game a name. Share the game code with your friend.

2

Find a Game

Use the game code or game name from your friend to join their created game. You'll see all the images they've selected.

3

Make Your Choice

Carefully examine each image. Choose and click on the image you will be playing as.

4

Ask Questions

Take turns asking yes/no questions about the opponent's selected image.
